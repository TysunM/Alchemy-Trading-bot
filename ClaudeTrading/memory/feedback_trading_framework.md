**The Rule:** Never buy "once we decide to trade something." Always seek the best analytical entry price. Patience on entry is the edge.

**Why:** Most traders chase the move and get stopped out on noise. You wait for convergence of independent signals (political cluster + top 1% traders + macro catalyst + technical confluence) at the BEST analyzed price, resulting in:
- Fewer, higher-quality trades
- Better entry prices (lower risk per trade)
- Higher win rate on each position
- Compound outperformance through patience

**How to apply:**

1. **The 3 Non-Negotiable Indicators** (check BEFORE every entry):
   - **Squeeze Momentum Indicator:** Bollinger Bands + Keltner Channels → identifies squeeze release (breakout timing)
   - **Advanced Multi-RSI Momentum System:** Weighted RSI (7/14/21/28/35) with zero-lag → confirms momentum direction + strength + fading zones
   - **Volume Acceptance Bands (VAB):** Statistical volume anomaly detection → shows Acceptance/Expansion/Mean-Revert states

2. **Fibonacci Integration:**
   - Base range = consolidation high to low
   - Entry zones = 38.2%, 50%, 61.8% retracements from low
   - Stop = 78.6% retracement (maximum pain tolerance)
   - Targets = 127.2%, 161.8%, 200% extensions

3. **Bollinger Bands Logic:**
   - Squeeze phase = consolidation WITHIN bands (waiting)
   - Release = price breaks OUTSIDE bands on volume expansion
   - Best entry = pullback to middle band (20 MA) after upper band break
   - Stop = below lower band or Fibonacci 78.6% (whichever is tighter)

4. **Pre-Entry Checklist (ALL must be ✅):**
   - Squeeze releasing NOW (bands expanding)?
   - Multi-RSI confirmed (not just early signal)?
   - Volume expanding above recent average?
   - Entry at Fibonacci confluence?
   - Political cluster / top 1% trader signal present?
   - Macro catalyst aligned?
   - Risk/reward 3x+ minimum?
   - Entering at best analyzed price, not chasing?

5. **Trade Selection Requires 3+ Layers:**
   - Capitol Trades cluster signal (multiple lawmakers, different parties)
   - TradingView top 1% already in position
   - Macro catalyst confirmed
   - Technical confluence (Fibonacci + Bollinger + Volume)
   - Historical volume profile support

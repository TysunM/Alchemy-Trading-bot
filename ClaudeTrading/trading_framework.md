## THE 3 CORE INDICATORS — Non-Negotiable Pre-Trade Checklist

Based on deep analysis of TradingView community + built-in indicators, these 3 work best with your strategy (Fibonacci + Bollinger Bands + volume + breakout confirmation):

### 🎯 INDICATOR #1: THE SQUEEZE MOMENTUM INDICATOR
**What it does:** Combines Bollinger Bands + Keltner Channels to identify periods of low volatility ("squeezes") that precede explosive price moves.

**Why it works for you:**
- Directly integrates Bollinger Bands (your preference)
- Shows compressed consolidation → breakout timing
- Visual color-coded signals for squeeze entry/expansion
- Reduces false breakout entries

**How to use it:**
1. Watch for the squeeze (bands tightening, low volatility)
2. When squeeze releases (bands expand outward), confirm with volume
3. Enter on the FIRST candle showing explosive momentum out of the squeeze
4. Do NOT enter during the squeeze itself

**Pre-trade check:** Is the squeeze releasing RIGHT NOW? If yes, proceed to check #2.

---

### 🎯 INDICATOR #2: ADVANCED MULTI-RSI MOMENTUM SYSTEM
**What it does:** Weighted combination of multiple RSI lengths (7, 14, 21, 28, 35) with zero-lag smoothing to reduce false signals and show early momentum shifts.

**Why it works for you:**
- Reduces noise vs. single RSI (which your bot already uses)
- Shows momentum DIRECTION + STRENGTH + FADING zones
- Color-coded background = clear visual entry/exit signals
- Catches early momentum before the move fully develops

**How to use it:**
1. Look for the color-coded background transitioning from bearish → early bullish
2. Wait for confirmation color (full bullish signal, not just early)
3. Cross-reference with Squeeze: is momentum expanding as Bollinger Bands expand?
4. If both align = HIGH CONFIDENCE entry zone

**Pre-trade check:** Is Multi-RSI showing confirmed momentum (not early signal)? If yes, proceed to check #3.

---

### 🎯 INDICATOR #3: VOLUME ACCEPTANCE BANDS (VAB)
**What it does:** Statistical volume anomaly detection identifying price levels where the market has "accepted" a range, then tracks what happens when that acceptance breaks (Acceptance, Expansion, or Mean-Revert states).

**Why it works for you:**
- Volume is non-negotiable for confirming breakouts
- Shows which breakouts have real conviction (volume expansion) vs. fakes (volume decline)
- Identifies optimal entry price WITHIN the move (expansion phase vs. fading)
- Prevents chasing weak moves

**How to use it:**
1. Identify current acceptance level (where volume is concentrated)
2. Watch for expansion state (volume ABOVE recent average)
3. Only enter when the stock breaks acceptance ON VOLUME EXPANSION
4. If volume declining while price breakouts = FAKE, skip trade

**Pre-trade check:** Is volume expanding as price breaks out of acceptance level? If yes = GO. If no = WAIT.

## FIBONACCI INTEGRATION
**How Fibonacci works in your framework:**

1. **Identify the base range** (highest swing high to lowest swing low of the consolidation)
2. **Calculate Fibonacci retracements:** 0%, 23.6%, 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%, 78.6%, 100%
3. **Best entry zones:** 38.2% or 50% retracement from the base LOW (pullback entry after initial breakout)
4. **Stop loss placement:** Below the 78.6% retracement (maximum pain tolerance)
5. **Take profit zones:** Next Fibonacci extension level above breakout (127.2%, 161.8%, 200%)

**For MU specifically:**
- Base consolidation: $379 (March low) to $471.25 (ATH)
- Range size: $92.25
- Fibonacci levels from $379:
  - 38.2% retrace = $379 + ($92.25 × 0.382) = **$414.27**
  - 50% retrace = $379 + ($92.25 × 0.50) = **$425.13**
  - 61.8% retrace = $379 + ($92.25 × 0.618) = **$436.00**

## BOLLINGER BANDS ENTRY LOGIC
**Standard Bollinger Bands (20-period, 2 standard deviations):**

1. **Pre-breakout**: Price consolidating within bands = squeeze phase
2. **Breakout signal**: Price closes OUTSIDE the upper band on volume
3. **Best entry price**: Price pulls back to the MIDDLE BAND (20 MA) after breaking above upper band
   - This is where Fibonacci + Bollinger Bands converge
4. **Stop loss**: Below lower band or below Fibonacci 78.6% — whichever is tighter

**For MU specifically:**
- If MU breaks above $402.15 MA on volume:
   - Upper Bollinger Band resistance: ~$410-$415
   - Middle Band (20 MA): ~$402-$406
   - Ideal entry: Price pulls back to touch 20 MA while staying above $402.15 breakout level
   - This = best risk/reward within the move

## TRADE EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### STAGE 1: RESEARCH PHASE
1. Identify candidate stock (political cluster, top trader consensus, or macro catalyst)
2. Run all three indicators on daily + 4H timeframes
3. Calculate Fibonacci levels
4. Set Bollinger Bands (20, 2SD) and volume profile
5. Document entry price zones (Fibonacci confluences)

### STAGE 2: WAITING PHASE
- Monitor the stock DAILY
- Squeeze Momentum for release signal

- **Do NOT enter until all three indicators align**

### STAGE 3: ENTRY PHASE
- Price breaks key level (Fibonacci or Bollinger Band) on volume expansion
- All three indicators show CONFIRMED signals (not early, not trailing)
- Entry price is at or near a Fibonacci retracement level
- Execute position size: 50% immediately, 50% on confirmed sustained move

### STAGE 4: MANAGEMENT PHASE
- Stop loss: Fibonacci 78.6% or lower Bollinger Band (whichever is tighter)
- Target 1: Fibonacci extension (127.2% from base)
- Target 2: Next Fibonacci extension (161.8%)
- Target 3: Full moonshot target (200%+ extension)
- Partial profit-takes at each target zone
